My Why

A journey rooted in personal transformation.

My own journey began with personal transformation and grew through certifications in Clutter Clearing, Feng Shui, Reiki, and Life Mastery Coaching. Along with a degree in Health Education and decades of experience, I bring a natural sensitivity to the energy of people and spaces. Whether you're downsizing, navigating a life transition, or simply craving peace and clarity in your home, I'm here to help you reconnect with both your space and yourself.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of walking with families as they released decades of belongings and transforming homes that once felt heavy or chaotic into places that feel light, clear, and alive. My work is never just about tidying up — it's about creating flow, healing relationships, and restoring balance.
